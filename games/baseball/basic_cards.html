Basic Cards

The professor has calculated a complete set of basic cards for the 1970 and 2000 seasons.

  • 2000 American League Batting and Pitching Cards
  • 2000 National League Batting and Pitching Cards
  • 1970 American League Batting and Pitching Cards
  • 1970 National League Batting and Pitching Cards

If you need a batting card for a pitcher who does not have one, use the following generic card. 

GENERIC PITCHER
HITTING:
     000 Strike Out
     791 Hit Out
     999 Walk
Thus your pitchers will bat more like Gaylord Perry than Bob Gibson.
