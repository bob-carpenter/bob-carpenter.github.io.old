Bob Carpenter's Erdös Number

My Erdös number is less than or equal to 4. The Erdös number is determined by stringing together collaborators on published papers.

  • 0. Paul Erdös
  • 1. Kenneth Kunen
  • 2. K. Jon Barwise
  • 3. Rich Thomason
  • 4. Bob Carpenter

That is, Paul Erdös wrote a paper with Kenneth Kunen, who wrote a paper with Jon Barwise, who wrote a paper with Rich Thomason, with whom I wrote a paper.

Thanks to Chris Volinsky's DBLP Social Network Application, which helped me shave two links off the upper bound.
